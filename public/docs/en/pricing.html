Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I exceed my monthly limit?

You will receive a notification when you reach 80% of your limit. If you exceed it, you can purchase additional credits or upgrade your plan at any time.

Can I change plans at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan whenever you need. Changes will apply on your next billing cycle.

What do I get with the Source Code?

You receive the full Git repository (Frontend and Backend). You are free to modify, adapt, and host it anywhere you prefer. The license allows commercial use for one business instance.

Do prices include taxes?

Prices shown are in USD and do not include local taxes. VAT or sales tax will be calculated based on your location at payment.
